About Us

Savory Restaurant was introduced in 1950 by the Ting brothers. avory Chicken is known as its distinctive tasty, juicy and tender chicken. Aside from the Savory Chickem, the menu also includes other popular Chinese dishes. After over 50 years, the third generation of the Ting family who opened the first Savory branch in Escolta are re-launching the branch via Classic Savory mall outlets so that more of the younger generations can continue to Savory this unique fried chicken and its other specialities. Classic Savory is scheduled to open more branches in global this year to serve our growing clients.
